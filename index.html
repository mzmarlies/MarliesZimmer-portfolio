about me.

I am a front-end web developer with a background in making animal simulation models for veterinary schools. This experience honed my abilities to think critically, analyze complex problems, work efficiently under pressure, and work well independently as well as with a team.

My favourite things to do in my spare time are rock climbing and bouldering. I also enjoy playing music (piano, guitar, and the harp when I'm able to rent one), learning French and Spanish, and painting with acrylics.
