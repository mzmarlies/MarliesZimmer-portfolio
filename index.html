about me.

I am a front-end web developer with a background in making animal simulation models for veterinary schools where I honed my abilities to think critically, analyze complex problems, work efficiently under pressure, and work well independently as well as with a team.

I have experience writing clean, accessible code using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScipt and React and I enjoy working on both paired and solo projects. I love meeting new people and I bring a positive, determined, and calm energy to all the work that I do.

My greatest passion outside of coding is rock climbing and bouldering. I also enjoy playing music (piano, guitar, and harp), reading, and learning languages (French and Spanish).
